# Example, lesson opener

The opening card for Lesson 1 of "Connecting AI Agents to Systems".

## Files

| File | What it is |
|---|---|
| `LessonOpener.example.tsx` | The authored opener: course title + lesson number + headline, with the reveal sequence. |

## What it shows

The card builds in one beat: background fades in (`setup`), then the course logo row (`logo`), the "Lesson One" eyebrow (`label`), the headline "From Map to Connection" (`title`), and the brand badge (`badge`). As a title card it carries a single welcome/framing voiceover over the whole build rather than a cue per element.
